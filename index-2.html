4 steps to the perfect trip

Your Zicasso trip planning begins the minute you submit our private trip request form. Using sophisticated technology and in-depth human know-how, we personally identify 2-3 travel companies that are the most knowledgeable and qualified to craft your specific vacation. They then compete to concoct the perfect vacation recipe — with a few little sweeteners thrown in!

  • You tell us the destinations, dates and preferred ingredients of your dream trip.
  • We match you with 2-3 top travel specialists and link them (and only them) to your private information at Zicasso.
  • They compete for your business, offering expert advice and developing detailed proposals.
  • You purchase the ideal itinerary after it’s refined to perfection with you.
